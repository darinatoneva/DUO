Tante Musika

Christine [about herself]

I’ve lived half of my life at DR as a kids- and girlchoir singer, TV host and actress. As a kid I performed with Danmarks Underholdningsorkester - both as a solo artist and a choir singer. I am a big fan of the orchestra! Besides this I am an educated singer for the Royal Danish Music Conservatory and have worked as a singer and actress for many years. I have been a host at family and school concerts with Århus Symphony Orchestra, Odense Symphony Orchestra and DR Symphony Orchestra. The last 15 years I have worked as a singing and choir teacher at Glostrup Music School as well as a teacher of music and rhythm at schools in Roskilde municipality. I have been the host on Kaj and Andrea for 12 years and am also a children’s book author, songwriter, actress and host at Carlsen Kids. But first and foremost I am a singer and musician - a life without music is unthinkable for me.
